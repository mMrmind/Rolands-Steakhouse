Our Heritage & Story

Founded in 1994, Roland's Steak House began with a simple mission: to bring an authentic, world-class steakhouse experience to General Santos City. For decades, we have dedicated ourselves to sourcing only the highest quality meats, aging them to perfection, and grilling them exactly to your liking.

Our master chefs combine traditional fire-grilling techniques with modern culinary innovations, ensuring every bite is a celebration of flavor.
